Before I mentioned I'm a private pilot. Here's a little more about that. My aviation journey began when I was 13 in the Royal Air Force Air Cadets, where I had an Air Experience Flight in a Grob Tutor. After having looked skyward for the majority of my life, having experienced that Air Experience Flight left me with a desire to fly.

In December of 2019, I was awarded for my efforts in aviation specifically in the Royal Air Force Air Cadets, with a 12 hour fully funded flying scholarship in Dundee, Scotland.
